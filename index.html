About Me

Hello! I am a PhD Candidate in the School of Math at the University of Minnesota. My thesis research is in bifurcations in thermohaline circulations, representative of the Atlantic Meridional Overturning Circulation (AMOC). Other projects have been in the world of Topological Data Anlysis applied to Indian Monsoon systems and data analysis of cancerous cell populations. During my PhD program, I have also developed as a math educator, a math community coordinator, and a ceramicist.
